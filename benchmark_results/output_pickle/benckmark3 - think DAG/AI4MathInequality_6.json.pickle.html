Informal theorem and proof
Theorem: Prove that for all real numbers $x, y, z$, the following inequality holds: $x^{4}+y^{4}+z^{4} \geq 4 x y z - 1$. Proof: For all real $x,y,z$, we have $x^4+y^4+z^4-4xyz+1 = (x^2-1)^2 + (y^2-z^2)^2 + 2(yz-x)^2 \ge 0$,since squares are non-negative. Therefore, $x^4+y^4+z^4-4xyz+1 \ge 0$.
