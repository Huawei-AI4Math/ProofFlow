Informal theorem and proof
Theorem: If $A \subseteq B$ and $B \subseteq C$, then $A \subseteq C$. Proof: Let $x \in A$. Since $A \subseteq B$, we have $x \in B$. Since $B \subseteq C$, we have $x \in C$. Therefore $A \subseteq C$.
